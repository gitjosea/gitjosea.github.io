Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Your privacy is our highest priority. bwsMemKeeper is designed from the ground up to protect your personal information.

  • Data Storage: bwsMemKeeper operates strictly on an offline-first configuration. All log data, drink cards, tasting notes, and ratings are stored 100% locally on your physical device.
  • Data Collection & Transmission: The application does not collect, track, transmit, or monitor any personal user data, usage metrics, or location details. No data ever leaves your physical device or is shared with third-party entities.
